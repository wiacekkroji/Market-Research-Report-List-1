<p><strong>The Compressor Start Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heavy Hammer Start Relay</li><li>PTC Start Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compressor start relay market is primarily divided into two types: Heavy Hammer Start Relay and PTC Start Relay. Heavy Hammer Start Relays utilize a mechanical mechanism to engage the compressor, providing high starting torque, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, PTC Start Relays use positive temperature coefficient thermistors to initiate the start, offering a more compact and efficient design. These relays play a crucial role in improving the reliability and performance of refrigeration and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
