<p><p>The Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers market is segmented into two primary types: Flame Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometer (FAAS) and Electrothermal Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometer (ETAAS). FAAS utilizes a flame to vaporize samples, making it suitable for analyzing higher concentrations of metals in liquids. In contrast, ETAAS employs a graphite furnace for sample analysis, allowing for the detection of trace elements with higher sensitivity and precision. Both technologies are essential in various fields, including environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and metallurgy.</p></p>
